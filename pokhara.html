Pokhara

7 days Medium treehouse trek

Day 1: Arrival in Pokhara

Morning: Arrive in Pokhara and check into your accommodation. Afternoon: Explore Lakeside Pokhara. Take a stroll along Phewa Lake and enjoy the views of the Annapurna Range. Evening: Dinner at one of the lakeside restaurants.

Day 2: Sarangkot Sunrise and Devi's Fall

Early Morning: Drive to Sarangkot for a sunrise view of the Annapurna Range. Morning: Visit the International Mountain Museum. Afternoon: Explore Devi's Fall and Gupteshwor Cave. Evening: Relax by the lake or enjoy some shopping in Lakeside.

Day 3: Boating in Phewa Lake and Peace Pagoda

Morning: Take a boat ride on Phewa Lake. Visit Tal Barahi Temple situated on an island in the lake. Afternoon: Hike or take a taxi to the World Peace Pagoda for panoramic views of Pokhara and the surrounding mountains. Evening: Leisure time in Lakeside

Day 4:Short Hike and Departure

Morning: Take a short hike to the Peace Stupa or explore the local market. Afternoon: Depending on your departure time, you may have some free time for last-minute shopping or relaxation. Departure: Head to the airport for your departure.
